NPS Act 2020 compliance

As required by Bank of Uganda, orders of UGX 1,000,000 or above require identity verification via national ID, passport, or alien ID before payment is processed. Funds are held in escrow by a licensed payment service provider until delivery is confirmed — Omuzubuzi never holds your money directly.
